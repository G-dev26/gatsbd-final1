SWEET KISSES

In every kiss, I felt that you were mine.
Your lips — they were always perfect with mine,
like they were meant to find me in this lifetime.

You have no idea how I felt each time you kissed me.
Like time stopped.
Like nothing else mattered but you.
Every time I looked into your eyes, my heart whispered that you were the one.

Those kisses told stories my heart was too afraid to speak.
Maybe that's why I kissed you more than you kissed me —
because I thought it was the only way I could say "I love you."

But now, they've become memories I'll hold onto,
no matter how much it hurts.

And God, how I wish I could kiss you again.
Just once.
Just to feel that warmth, that safety, that love —
even for a second.

But I can't.
Because you're not mine anymore.
You're not my baby anymore… and it hurts.
And now, I have to learn to live with that.

Still...
something deep inside me keeps whispering —
it might be you.
It's always been you.

I'm sorry, love.
For all the things I couldn't be.
For all the love that's still here, with nowhere to go.
