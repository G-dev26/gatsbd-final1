WARM HUGS

In your arms, I found a sanctuary.

Your hugs felt like home —
the safest, warmest place I've ever known.
Whenever you held me close, the world softened.
I always ended up falling asleep in your embrace,
because that's how safe I felt with you.

Even when I laid on your lap,
or stayed still in your arms on those cold chairs,
I never wanted to move — because being next to you felt like peace.

I'll miss the softness of your skin,
how it calmed me like a quiet lullaby against my own restless heart.
And your scent… God, your scent.
That familiar fragrance that clung to my clothes,
that stayed on me after every hug, every sleep, every morning —
even before you'd showered.
It was you. It's always been you.

I'll miss everything about those hugs —
the way they healed me without saying a word,
the way they made me believe I belonged somewhere.

You were my safest place to fall.
And now, I carry that emptiness like a quiet ache.

— Forever in your arms
